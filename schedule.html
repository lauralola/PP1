Our Teachers

Tara Scullen

With over 15 years of practice both in traditional hatha and flow yoga, Tara has a gentle and purposeful approach yoga. Tara leads our Monday, Wednesday and Friday evening practice at the studio.

James Morris

James trained in India and has been practicing for over 20 years. Bringing a spiritual aspect to his classes with an intention to find more openness in the body. James leads our Tuesday and Thursday practice

Molly Hayden

Molly trained here in Dublin with the wonderful Stuart Jackson. Her practice brings lightness and fun to the classes. Molly teaches our Saturday practice and leads a sea swim after our early meditation class each week.
